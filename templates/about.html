About Us

Conscious Luxury. Permanent Style.

Arabela is a gown rental boutique that believes every occasion deserves something extraordinary — without the permanence of ownership. We curate high-quality gowns for weddings, debuts, proms, and every milestone in between.

Founded with a passion for accessible luxury, Arabela brings designer-quality pieces within reach. Our collection is carefully maintained so every gown arrives in pristine condition, ready for your most memorable moments.

We are committed to personalized service — from selection to fitting, we guide you every step of the way.

Our Mission

To make every woman feel confident and beautiful by offering premium gown rentals at accessible prices.
