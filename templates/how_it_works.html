How It Works

Renting with Arabela is simple. Five steps, zero stress.

Step 01
search

Find Your Pieces

Browse our collection for your rental date. Filter by size and style to find your perfect look — from wedding gowns to filipinianas and everything in between.

Step 02

Add to Cart

Select your pieces and choose a rental period. Need more time than the standard window? Send us an email and we'll work it out together.

Step 03

Pick Up

Visit our boutique on your scheduled pickup date. Our team will have everything ready and do a final fitting to make sure everything is perfect before you leave.

Step 04
celebration

Wear & Enjoy

Make your moment. Don't forget to snap some photos — we'd love to see them and hear how the night went!

Step 05
refresh

Return

Bring your pieces back by the agreed return date in their original condition. Once our team inspects the items, your security deposit is fully refunded.
