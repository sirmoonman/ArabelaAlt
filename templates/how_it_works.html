How It Works

Step 02

Once you've selected your pieces, book a rental period. If you need the rental for more than the allotted time, send us an email to make the request.

Step 03

Pickup

Visit our boutique on your scheduled pickup date. Our staff will have your pieces ready and do a final check to make sure everything fits perfectly before you leave.

Bring your pieces back by the agreed return date in their original condition. Once our team inspects the items, your security deposit is fully refunded.
